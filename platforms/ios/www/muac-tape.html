MUAC Tape

Mid-Upper Arm Circumference Tape

Along with other assessments, the MUAC tape is a simple tool use for quickly measuring the upper arm of children aged between six months and five years old to find out if they are malnourished. There are 3 colour zones on the tape, each representing a nutritional status.

Red (≤115mm)

Severe Malnutrition

The child should be immediately referred for treatment.

Yellow (115 – 125mm)

Moderate Malnutrition

The child should be immediately referred for supplementation and is at risk of severe acute malnutrition.

Green (≥125mm)

No apparent risk of Malnutrition

The child is well nourished.
